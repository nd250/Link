Imbuga Zikomeye ku Isi ku bijyanye n’Imirire Myiza

  • World Health Organization (WHO) – Nutrition – Iha inama mpuzamahanga zishingiye ku bushakashatsi ku mirire.
  • UNICEF – Nutrition – Iteza imbere imirire y’abana n’abagore mu bihugu bitandukanye.
  • EatRight.org (Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics) – Iha abantu inama z’indyo yuzuye, imirire y’abana, ababyeyi, n’abarwaye.
  • American Society for Nutrition (ASN) – Itegura ubushakashatsi n’amakuru agezweho ku byerekeye imirire.
  • Healthline – Nutrition – Iyi site itanga inyandiko zizewe ku ndyo, ibiribwa bifite akamaro, no kubungabunga ubuzima.
  • Harvard T.H. Chan – The Nutrition Source – Ubushakashatsi buhanitse bwakozwe n’abahanga b’i Harvard ku mirire myiza.

    Ubuzima bwo mu mutwe

    Gahunda z’ubuzima bwo mu mutwe kugira ngo wirinde guhangayika no kurushaho kuba mu mwuka mwiza.

    Imbuga zo mu Rwanda zifasha ku buzima bwo mu mutwe

    • RBC – Mental Health Division – Igice cya RBC cyita ku buzima bwo mu mutwe, harimo gahunda yo kwita ku bafite ihungabana n’ihungabana ryo mu gihe cya nyuma y’intambara.
    • MentalHealth.rw – Urubuga rwihariye ku buzima bwo mu mutwe, rufasha abantu kumenya no gusaba ubufasha.
    • IGIHE – Ubuzima bwo mu mutwe – Inyandiko nyinshi ziri mu Kinyarwanda ku bijyanye n’ubuzima bwo mu mutwe.

    Imbuga mpuzamahanga ku buzima bwo mu mutwe

    • WHO – Mental Health – Inama mpuzamahanga z’Ubuyobozi bw’Isi ku buzima bwo mu mutwe.
    • Mental Health Foundation (UK) – Inama n’ubumenyi bugezweho ku buryo bwo kubungabunga ubuzima bwo mu mutwe.
    • NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness) – Ubuhamya, inkunga n’ubumenyi ku bantu bafite ihungabana.
    • Psychology Today – Inyandiko nyinshi zanditswe n’abahanga mu mitekerereze n’imyitwarire y’abantu.

    Kwivuza

    Inama ku bijyanye n'ubuvuzi, uburyo bwo kubaho neza no kwirinda indwara.

    Amavuriro yagufasha mubijyanye n'ubuzima mu Rwanda

    • King Faisal Hospital – Kigali
      Ibitaro byitiriwe umwami faisal
      Serivisi zita ku ndwara zo mu mutwe, harimo kuvura ihungabana, indwara zo kwiheba, na bipolar.
    • Ibitaro bya Ndera (Caraes Ndera Hospital) – Ndera, Kigali
      Ibitaro bya Ndera
      Ibitaro bikuru by'indwara zo mu mutwe mu Rwanda, bitanga ubuvuzi bw'inzobere ku ndwara zo mu mutwe.
    • Icyizere Psychotherapeutic Center – Kicukiro, Kigali
      Icyizere Physicotherapeutic Center
      Kuvura ihungabana, ibiyobyabwenge, n'ubujyanama ku bibazo byo mu mutwe.
    • Ibitaro bya Nyarugenge (Nyarugenge District Hospital) – Kigali
      Ibitaro bya Nyarugenge
      Isuzuma n'ubuvuzi bw'indwara zitandukanye zo mu mutwe, bifatanyije n'abandi baganga b'inzobere.
    • Solid Minds Counseling Clinic – Kigali
      Solid Minds Counseling
      Ubujyanama ku giti cy'umuntu, imiryango, n'amatsinda, hamwe na gahunda zo kubaka ubushobozi.

      Mental Health Hub
      Gushyigikira ibikorwa by'abafatanyabikorwa mu iterambere, imiryango itari iya leta, n'ibigo by'abikorera mu bijyanye n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe.

      partners in health
